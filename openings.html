Openings

Interested applicants with a strong command of AI and health data science may apply. Please Send an email to either ahmad.pahlavan@maine.eduor sharon.watterson@maine.edu, and add your recent CV.

The HexAI offers the following to USM Undergraduate and Graduate Research Assistants

Potential benefits include but not limited to:

  • Working with world-class leaders in health data science research and a multidisciplinary team of computer scientists, health informaticians, statisticians, physicians, and surgeons.
  • Developing mentoring relationships with faculty member(s) and scientists.
  • Working on real-world, clinically significant, and highly practical AI applications. It opens the door to the students(s) to pursue their professional career in AI-Powered healthcare.
  • Networking with professional communities in the healthcare domain and leveraging real clinical data.
  • Participating in educational/training opportunities provided frequently by the center.
  • Access to high-performace computational resources.
